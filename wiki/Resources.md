# Seminar Resources

A curated list of essential papers, books, guides, and videos covering social insects, movement ecology, agent-based modeling, and python programming.

## 🐜 Social Insects
### Building Behaviour
- **Theraulaz et al. 2003.** *The formation of spatial patterns in social insects: from simple behaviours to complex structures.* Phil. Trans. R. Soc. Lond. A. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1098/rsta.2003.1198)
- **Perna & Theraulaz. 2017.** *When social behaviour is moulded in clay: on growth and form of social insect nests.* J. Exp. Biol. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1242/jeb.143347)
- **Khuong et al. 2016.** *Stigmergic construction and topochemical information shape ant nest architecture.* PNAS. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1509829113)

### Self-Organisation
- **Theraulaz & Bonabeau. 1999.** *A Brief History of Stigmergy.* Artificial life. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1162/106454699568700)
- **Bonabeau et al. 1997.** *Self-organization in social insects.* Trends in Ecology & Evolution. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/S0169-5347(97)01048-3)

## 📍 Movements in Ants
- **Richardson et al. 2022.** *Two simple movement mechanisms for spatial division of labour in social insects.* Nat Commun. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-34706-7)
- **Khuong et al. 2013.** *How Do Ants Make Sense of Gravity? A Boltzmann Walker Analysis of Lasius niger Trajectories on Various Inclines.* PLOS ONE. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0076531)
- **Fourcassié & Traniello. 1994.** *Food searching behaviour in the ant Formica schaufussi: response of naive foragers to protein and carbohydrate food.* Animal Behaviour. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1006/anbe.1994.1212)
- **Schultheiss & Cheng. 2012.** *Finding food: outbound searching behavior in the Australian desert ant Melophorus bagoti.* Behavioral Ecology. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1093/beheco/ars143)
- **Casellas et al. 2007.** *From individual to collective displacements in heterogeneous environments.* J. theor. Biol. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jtbi.2007.10.011)
